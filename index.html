  5 - How to mint ?

  First, make sure you have enough $ETH in your wallet and that your wallet is on the correct mainnet (Ethereum's mainnet).

  By simply clicking the Mint button, you will be redirected to the minting page and pressing "connect to metamask" to log into your Metamask wallet, select the amount and click the buy button.

  If Metamask is installed correctly, a pop-up window will open and you just need to confirm the transaction. You can adjust the gas limit according to the network situation but it is not recommended to significantly lower the gas limit to avoid a transaction error.

  After the mint is finished, you can directly view your NFT in Opensea at dogeinupunks collection and put it up for sale if you wish in the second market.
